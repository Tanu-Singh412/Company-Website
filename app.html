Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to develop a mobile app?

The time to develop an app depends on its complexity. Simple apps can take 3-6 months, while more complex apps with advanced features may take 6-12 months or longer.

Which platform should I develop for: iOS, Android, or both?

It depends on your target audience. If your audience is more diverse, it’s recommended to develop for both platforms. Cross-platform solutions like Flutter or React Native can help reach both iOS and Android users efficiently.

How much does it cost to develop a mobile app?

App development costs vary based on complexity, features, and platforms. A basic app might cost between $10,000 to $50,000, while more complex apps can go beyond $100,000.

Will you help me publish the app on the App Store or Google Play?

Yes, we handle the entire submission process for both the Apple App Store and Google Play Store, ensuring that your app meets all necessary guidelines.
