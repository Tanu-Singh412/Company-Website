What is a Bulk Voice SMS

Bulk voice SMS is a service that allows businesses to send pre-recorded voice messages to a large number of recipients simultaneously.

How do Bulk Voice SMS work?

Bulk Voice SMS is a way to send pre-recorded voice messages to many people at once. It’s often used for marketing, alerts, reminders, political campaigns, and engaging with customers. Here’s how it usually works: a business records a message, uploads it to the service, and then selects the audience to receive the call. The service then dials each number and plays the message, ensuring everyone gets the information quickly and clearly.

  • A business records a message.
  • The message is uploaded to the voice SMS service.
  • The target audience is selected.
  • The service dials each number and plays the message.
